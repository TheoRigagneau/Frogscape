# FrogScape
> FrogScape is a no code 2D platformer built around a variety of themed levels, each introducing its own unique gameplay mechanics. The game, released during a 2‑day game jam, includes multiple tutorials to help players understand and master these mechanics as they progress.

## Gameplay Overview
FrogScape combines classic platforming with mechanics inspired by iconic game universes. Each level introduces a new way to interact with the environment, encouraging players to adapt, experiment, and explore.

## Core Mechanics
- Double Jump (Dofus Level)
Allows the player to perform a second jump mid-air to reach higher platforms or cross wide gaps.
- Gravity Control (Undertale Level)
Lets the player switch gravity to walk on ceilings or floors.
Special stars can also disable certain objects, adding puzzle-solving elements.
- Dash (Space Bar)
Grants a quick burst of speed to dodge obstacles or reach distant platforms.
- Portal Teleportation (Portal Level)
Enables the player to teleport between portals to navigate complex level layouts.
- Pokémon Capture & Snorlax Placement (Pokémon Levels)
Players can capture Pokémon to obtain Pokéballs.
Using the Shift key, they can place Snorlax to create temporary platforms and access normally unreachable areas.

## Level Themes
- Dofus — Focus on aerial mobility with double jumps.
- Undertale — Gravity manipulation and object-disabling stars.
- Portal — Teleportation puzzles and spatial logic.
- Pokémon — Capture mechanics and strategic Snorlax placement.

## Tutorials
Each mechanic is introduced through a dedicated tutorial level, ensuring players understand how to use it before encountering more complex challenges.

## Project Goals
- Provide a fun and varied platforming experience.
- Encourage experimentation through diverse mechanics.
- Offer levels inspired by beloved game universes while keeping original gameplay.
